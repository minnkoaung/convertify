WHAT'S CONVERTIFY

Solution for voice recording conversations to be translated and converted into documents such as reports/ contract agreements/ billing invoices/ Updates to an internal system module and parameters.

HOWREGULATE

A pre set template format of documents that will be use in your lifestyle was defined. Eg. service contract for engagement of contractor, Tution teacher agreement template, freelance music teacher agreement.

In every parameters of the selected inputs of the template that will be generated into the format of your document.

Voice recognition during the conversation was activated during the conversation recording when “End and deal “ is tapped.

HOWOPERATE

Dashboard: Highlights of latest activities in each module as a homepage summary, Service Provider, Events, Tasks, Projects, Instructions, Business, Enterprise Resource Planning, Notification, Users, Reports
