Recording order

Once customer voice stopped speaking for 15 seconds, it will automatically prompt “ done “. Once the system prompt done, customer may manually touch on the screen “ done “ to summarize a finished conversation order. If not finished, customer can continue speaking ad system will automatically switch to recording mode.
